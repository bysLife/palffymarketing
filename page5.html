Dear Business owner, 


Marketing and sales has always been known as being “sleazy”.

I feel anyone who says something along those lines - indicate that they simply don’t like and don’t believe in the product they are selling.

Instead they are doing it only for the money.


I never thought I'd ever enter into marketing or sales. I was on the road to becoming a chiropractor. 

I have an inborn knack for promoting businesses that I believe in. And I hunt out talent that functions on the same ground.


I look forward to working with you.
